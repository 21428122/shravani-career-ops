## General rules

- **Always attach the company-specific PDF** — not the base. The headline names the role, which reviewers notice.
- **Never send from bulk-messaging tools.** One-off, hand-sent.
- **Follow up once after 5 business days** if no reply. No more.
- **If ignored twice, move on.** Don't spam the PM team.
