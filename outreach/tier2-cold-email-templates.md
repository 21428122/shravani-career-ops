# Tier 2 — Cold Outreach Templates

3 modular templates. Use the one matching the company's niche.

**Format:** Email when you can find the address; otherwise LinkedIn DM. Always attach a company-specific PDF — if a tailored one doesn't exist, use the Base PDF but update the headline manually.

**Subject line rule:** short, specific, no marketing voice. Good examples:
- `PM intern inquiry — CSAT 72→87 lift at healthcare CRM`
- `NYU Tandon PM grad — low-code automation + 94% edge-AI`

## Rules of thumb

- **One email per company per month.** No more.
- **Personalize the trigger line** — reviewers filter for generic templates in 5 seconds.
- **Always CC a referral if one exists.** NYU alums on LinkedIn are the shortest path.
- **Track every send** in a simple sheet: company, contact, date, outcome. If you get > 20% reply rate you're doing great.
